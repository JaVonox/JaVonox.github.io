Pumpkin Panic was a 2019 gamejam project where two players fought to survive against an endless onslaught of evil jack o lanterns. A player may win by collecting 50 coins, dropped by the pumpkins as they are slain with the players scythes - or alternatively, a player can simply attempt to outlive their opponent. Each player begins the game with 50 "lives" - representing a single hit by a pumpkin, and without any means for a player to restore lives, they must play carefully as to not give their opponent an easy victory.

Pumpkin Panic was developed entirely in unity, with the entire game, assets included, developed by a small team within 48 hours.

Unfortunately, the repository for the project is private at the request of one of the team members, and therefore the source code nor download can be shared.
