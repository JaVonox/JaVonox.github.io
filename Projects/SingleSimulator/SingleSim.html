Single Simulator is a parody of alien horror games like "Signal Sim" and "Voices of the void" - inwhich the player takes control of a recent transfer to the signals department - Dr. Wester. Players are tasked with locating lonely souls in space and matching them up with the most appropriate alien - recieving credits to spend on equipment upgrades based on their compatability.

The game is built entirely in unity, and is in active development - plenty of new features will be added, including a main story and various new gameplay features and upgrades.
